The dark stone of the gate formed a path beneath his feet. In the shifting mists it was the only thing of any substance. Thoughe he knew that beyond the wall of fog was a vast land, other paths to wander, all else was swallowed by the haze. Even his conception of self seemed fluid and indistinct.

Sebastian thought of his child. Though there was little he could remember from the time when he lived with his parents, back then there had been many paths to wander, large clearings within the fog, that to a child, seemed vast and usizeable.
