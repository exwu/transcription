No one spoke as they made their way through the tunnels. The other recruits looked around curiously, no doubt astounded by the extent of these tunnels. But the guards in red marched on without much reaction; they had been here before. 

At last, they arrived at their destination. Several tunnels met here, opening up to a chamber only a little wider than the tunnels themselves.
