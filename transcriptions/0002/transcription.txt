"We should've taken more than just the kid."

"Nah, you saw what happened to Jeff. And that was just for pickin up some stray cash."

They exchanged brief banter now and then over staticky music played over the radio. For the most part they ignored their prisoner, sitting silent and blindfolded in a back seat. He had long since since pieced together the number of men in the car, associated names with voices, and promptly forgot them. As they drove through the night, he felt himself drift to and from sleep, waiting for them to feel the need to remove his cuffs and blindfold. 

Some hours later, the vehicle stopped.
