Suddenly he understood her nervousness. Sebastian had felt the discomfort himself, interacting with the ill, the insane, or the impoverish[ed]. A combination of pity and guilt. 

Sebastian put it from his mind. One day the cuts would heal, one day the ache would fade. One day Michelle would forget about him and he would forget about her. In the meanwhile, he could make sense of the past five years of suffering.
